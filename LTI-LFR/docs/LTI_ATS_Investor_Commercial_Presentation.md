# LTI ATS  
## Investor & Commercial Presentation

> **The intelligent recruitment platform that transforms hiring into a faster, more collaborative, and data-driven process.**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**LTI ATS** is a modern Applicant Tracking System designed to help companies **attract, evaluate, and hire talent more efficiently**.  
It combines **workflow automation, real-time collaboration, and AI assistance** to reduce operational friction and accelerate recruitment outcomes.

The platform is built around a clear value proposition:

- **Reduce hiring time**
- **Increase recruiter productivity**
- **Improve collaboration with hiring managers**
- **Standardize evaluation quality**
- **Deliver measurable operational efficiency**

LTI ATS is positioned as a **premium HR Tech SaaS solution** for organizations that want to modernize recruitment and scale hiring with control, speed, and intelligence.

---

## 2. Investment Thesis

The recruitment technology market continues to evolve from basic applicant tracking toward **intelligent talent operations platforms**.

LTI ATS is aligned with this shift because it goes beyond traditional ATS capabilities:

- It does not simply store candidates; it **orchestrates the entire hiring workflow**
- It reduces administrative overhead through **automation**

- It enables **shared execution between HR and business stakeholders**
- It creates a strong foundation for **recurring SaaS revenue and modular expansion**

## 7. Key Product Capabilities

## 7.1 Vacancy Management
Create, structure, approve, and manage job openings with reusable templates and configurable workflows.

## 7.2 Multi-Channel Job Publishing
Distribute jobs across career sites, job boards, and social channels from one centralized system.

## 7.3 Candidate Intake & Centralization
Capture all applications in one place with unified candidate profiles, CV parsing, and source tracking.

## 7.4 Screening & Review
Use structured pipelines, filters, scorecards, and AI-assisted summaries to prioritize candidates faster.

## 7.5 Online Testing
Integrate assessments and evaluations into the recruitment flow to improve pre-interview qualification.

## 7.6 Interview Coordination
Simplify scheduling, feedback collection, and cross-functional collaboration.

## 7.7 Selection & Hiring
Compare finalists, consolidate evaluation, and accelerate decision and offer management.
